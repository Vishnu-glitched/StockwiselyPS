StockWisely

StockWisely is a predictive platform that uses Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) to forecast future stock prices. It analyzes historical stock data, including open, close, high, low prices, and trading volume, along with technical indicators like RSI and EMA. By training a neural network on this data, StockWisely generates accurate predictions for future stock price movements. Users input historical data, and the platform provides predictions for a selected time period. Whether you're a beginner or experienced investor, StockWisely empowers you to make data-driven, informed decisions, helping you navigate the stock market with confidence.
